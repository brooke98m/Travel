Aesthetic & Architecture

Classic Haussmann

Built between 1853–1870 under Baron Haussmann's transformation of Paris. Characterized by ornate stone facades, wrought-iron balconies, herringbone parquet floors, decorative moldings, marble fireplaces, and a sense of timeless grandeur. Original period details are a strong plus — the older the bones, the better.

Period Details Ornate Moldings Marble Fireplace Herringbone Parquet Wrought Iron Stone Facade Grand Proportions

Deal-Breakers

  • ✕ Ground floor unit — no privacy, no light
  • ✕ Laminate or tile floors throughout (no original character)
  • ✕ North-facing only — must have natural light
  • ✕ Major structural renovation required
  • ✕ Building with significant deferred maintenance / co-ownership debt
Notes for My Agent

I am a US-based buyer purchasing as a second home and pied-à-terre. I am currently researching financing options and will confirm my structure before making an offer. I am serious and motivated — not a casual looker. My absolute priorities are light, ceiling height, and authentic Haussmann character. A beautifully renovated kitchen and updated bathrooms within a period building would be ideal. I am looking for 3 bedrooms, but am open to a spacious 2-bedroom if the layout and proportions are generous. Please flag anything with a functioning fireplace — that is a dream feature.
